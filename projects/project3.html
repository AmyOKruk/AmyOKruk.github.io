Live Election Results Maps, examples here and here.
For the 2020 elections, I was in charge of producing live results maps for a dozen NBC local markets across the U.S. showing the Presidential, Senate and House races. To do this, I wrote API queries to get real-time results from the Associated Press, set up automated updating, conducted testing and created the maps from scratch using D3.js. The maps detected the NBC market they were embedded in and adjusted the results accordingly.
Tools: Python, Jupyter Notebook, HTML, CSS, JavaScript (including D3.js)
