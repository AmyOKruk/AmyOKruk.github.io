Throughout the pandemic, I’ve worked on many projects and standalone charts visualizing COVID-19 for NBC's readers. Here’s a selection of that work:
Flights To and From Covid Hotspots
A story about Thanksgiving travel during the pandemic using data from thousands of flights that traveled between airports in areas with high COVID rates.
Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (D3.js), Python.
COVID-19 Vaccination Maps
This graphic shows COVID-19 vaccination rates at the county level, including a progress bar for the percentage of the population that's been fully vaccinated. This graphic also detects where the reader is to show their home state. Data is updated daily from the California Department of Public Health via a Python scraper.
Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (D3.js), Python.
When Will You Get the Vaccine?
This project was created in January 2021 during the initial vaccine rollout. The interactive asks the reader a series of questions and uses a stacked bar to visualize where they fell in line to get the vaccine.
Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (D3.js), Python.
The COVID-19 Variant Waves
This live-updating chart shows the COVID variants waves in the U.S., highlighting the emergence of Omicron. It also detects where a reader is to customize the states it shows as tabs. Data is from CoVariants, updated daily with a Python scraper.
Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (D3.js), Python.
