Many Toxic and Hazardous Waste Sites Fall Within Flood Zones
I assisted NBC Boston with an investigation into the toxic and hazardous waste sites located in flood hazard zones in Massachusetts by creating an interactive map using data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. The map was also aired on TV, driving traffic to their website.
Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Leaflet), QGIS
