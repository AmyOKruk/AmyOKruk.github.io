Many Counties Face Challenges as US Seeks to Reach Herd Immunity
We used data to show that the most vaccine hesitant counties in the U.S. also rated higher, on average, on an index measuring longstanding vaccine coverage barriers, such as historic under-vaccination and weak health care systems.Â To do this, I combined county-level data on vaccine hesitancy data from the Department of Health and Human Services with data from a non-profit that had calculated a county-level vaccine access coverage index. I also created the visuals, using a scatterplot, small multiples and a line chart to tell the story.
Tools: Python, Jupyter Notebook, HTML, CSS, JavaScript (including D3.js), Datawrapper
