Hi, I'm Amy!

I’m a journalist, developer and designer who likes to make things on the internet. ✨

Right now, I’m a visual journalist at CNN. I wear many hats on the Data & Graphics team, but one of my favorite things to do is use digital drawing to tell better stories. Otherwise, I’m a bit of a generalist, wrangling data and using maps, charts and whatever I can dream up to make visual stories shine. 📈

Previously, I studied data journalism at Columbia University’s Graduate School of Journalism. Before that, I was a words-only journalist in Toronto, Canada at the Globe and Mail. Way, way back I ran a daily student newspaper, which I remember fondly but my GPA does not. 🤷
